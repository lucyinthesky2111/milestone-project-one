Clutch Kings College

Basic Car Maintenance Course

This course will teach you the skills to maintain your own car at home. You will work on a fleet of cars owned by the college in our purpose-built, state-of-the-art workshop. This is a beginner level course, where you will undertake practical maintenance tasks under the guidance and supervision of our expert instructors. You will be provided with the valuable feedback you need to become proficient in each technique. At the end of the course, you will be assessed on your ability to effectively, and safely, carry out each maintenance task without instructor assistance.
