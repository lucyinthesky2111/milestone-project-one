Basic Car Maintenance Course

Course overview

During this course, you will learn how to carry out the following basic car maintenance tasks:

Wheel change

Fluid check and top up

Oil change and filter replacement

Wiper blade change

Battery test and replacement

Bulb change

Brake discs and pads replacement

Tyre safety checks
