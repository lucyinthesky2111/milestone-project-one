About us

“Clutch Kings College is a highly-respected training provider of automotive courses in the south-west of England. Established in 1987, we have been training world-class automotive professionals for nearly 40 years. All our instructors are qualified mechanics with a minimum of 5 years industry experience, working in automotive garages across the UK.”
